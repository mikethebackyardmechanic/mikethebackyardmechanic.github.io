Pre Viewing

Has it ever been in an accident?
Has it been repainted?
Have major maintenance items been completed?
Is it a clean title?
Have all recalls been completed?
What kind of payment do they accept?

Pre Inspection

Ask what is wrong with the car?
Is there is anything you should know?
Is there two keys?
Why are they are selling the car?

Exterior Inspection

The condition of the paint
Scratches, dents and fading
Panel alignment
Rust
Magnet on suspect areas
Lift and let go of each door
Shocks: the car should look level. Push down on each corner
Wheels: Push and pull to check for looseness

Trunk

Check seal for over spray
Paint should match exterior
No musky smell or dampness
Spare tire and jack
Rust under the spare tire
Spare tire has air

Tires

Wear evenly between the inside and outside
Tire sizes are all the same
Check air with a tire gauge

Under Carriage

No leaks
Oil splatter on the floor board or frame
Ball joints, tie rods, and CV joints (no grease, no tears or cracks)
Anything that looks missing, bent or broken
The frame is free from any obvious repairs
No leakage from the brake calipers or drums
The exhaust rust should be minimal
Tailpipe soot (should be dry and dark gray)

Engine Compartment

Engine should be cool
Look for leakage or spraying (oil or coolant)
Hoses and belts are supple
Oil (dark brown or black, not gritty)Dipstick (signs of burnt oil or discoloration)
Take off the oil cap, look inside the valve cover
Look inside the cap for signs of sludge, metal specks, looks gray, milky, or foaminess
Transmission fluid should be reddish, not brown, and smell like oil, with no “burnt” odor
Power steering fluid level
Brake fluid level
Coolant reservoir level
Check metal coolant cap for any silver or orange flakes

Interior

Smell the interior
Wear on the driver seat, steering wheel, and pedals
Power Windows
Window tint (bubbles, not too dark)
Power Mirrors
Power Locks
Radio
AC/Heat (all settings, all vents)
Navigation
Sunroof
Rear View Mirror (auto-dim)
Parking Sensors (beep in reverse)
Lights (glove box, map, dashboard, etc)
Emergency Brake Holds car
Heated Seats
Wipers and washer fluid work
All Gauges Work
AC Leak Tester
Mats - look underneath for water
Check the headliner for stains or sags

Seats

Air bags not deployed
Wear
Power move/tilt/lumbar function
Headrests function
Seat belts work

Lights

Head lights
Brights
Turn signals
Hazards
Backup lights
All lights, front and back

Test Drive

Pre Start (key on, engine off)

Check Engine Light
Oil Light

Cold Start

Engine lights should go off
Check tailpipe smoke color
Heater test
Should idle smoothly
Shift back and forth between Drive and Reverse

Pre Start

Driving

Listen suspension squeaks or clunks
Listen for engine ticks or knocks
No over-rev on acceleration

Brakes

No squeaks or grinding
Lightly press: no thumping
Medium Press: Should not pull the steering wheel
Hard press: ABS comes on

Front End Components

Let go of the steering wheel (car should go straight)
Quick turns
Turn over speed bump
Circles with steering cranked (CV joints)

Highway

Brakes don't thump
Cruise control
No wind noise from window or moonroof

Post Drive

No leaks
